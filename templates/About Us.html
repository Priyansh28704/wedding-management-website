About Us - WedForLife

Welcome to WedForLife

At WedForLife, we believe that every love story is unique, and we’re here to help you celebrate that love in the most unforgettable way. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we are committed to providing you with everything you need for a perfect wedding experience. Our goal is simple – to create moments that last a lifetime!

Our platform offers you a comprehensive range of wedding services, from personalized wedding planning to curated vendor recommendations. We understand the stress that comes with wedding planning, and that’s why we aim to simplify the process, making it seamless and enjoyable for every couple.

We are two passionate individuals who share the dream of making your wedding journey extraordinary. With a deep love for weddings and a belief in the magic of love, we’ve created WedForLife to be the ultimate space for couples to find everything they need to make their day as special as their love story.

Priyanshu Patidar

Founder & CEO

Priyanshu is the visionary behind WedForLife. With years of experience in event planning and an innate passion for creating magical moments, he decided to turn his love for weddings into a platform that helps couples plan their perfect day.

Nikita Gandhare

Co-Founder & Chief Creative Officer

Nikita brings a creative flair to WedForLife. As a wedding planner with an eye for detail and an expert in making dreams come true, she crafts experiences that reflect the personality and style of every couple.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide couples with a one-stop destination for all their wedding needs. From wedding planning, venue selection, vendor recommendations, to unique wedding ideas, we are committed to making your special day stress-free, joyous, and beautifully unforgettable.

We believe that every wedding is a reflection of the couple’s story, which is why we offer personalized services tailored to match your vision. We want to create an experience where you can celebrate your love with your closest friends and family in a meaningful and memorable way.

Join us at WedForLife, where every wedding is a unique adventure, and let us help you create memories that last forever!
